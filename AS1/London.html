London

About

London is not just the capital of the United Kingdom. It is also one of the largest and oldest cities in the world. The city has a legendary history filled with countless stories of different rulers, people and majestic buildings. There are so many fun facts about London that you will encounter when exploring here.

A quote from the website Visit Britain:

From the unique vintage markets of Portobello Road to the huge high street shops of Oxford Street, London is a shopper’s paradise. Food lovers will be spoilt for choice with every kind of cuisine.
